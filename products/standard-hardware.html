Standard Hardware

We carry U.S. and European aerospace fasteners to both standard and OEM specifications. Among the products we carry are bolts, wrenchable nuts and nut plates, screws high tensile fasteners, and studs as well as a comprehensive inventory of Boeing standards covering both commercial aircraft and military programs.We are an authorized stocking distributor of the products of major aerospace fastener manufacturers.

picture_as_pdf AEHI,Inc. Parts Catalog (.pdf) 720kb

We carry products to the following American and European standards:
  1. NAS
  2. AN
  3. MS
  4. BAC
  5. ABS
  6. AS
  7. ASNA
  8. ASNE
  9. DAN
  10. DIN
  11. EN
  12. MIL
Through knowledge and vast experience in aerospace-grade fasteners, we have expanded our offerings and expertise beyond these products. Some of the other components we carry include:
  1. Bearings
  2. Brackets
  3. Clamps
  4. Collars
  5. Complex machined components
  6. Composite hardware
  7. Connectors
  8. Electrical hardware
  9. Inserts
  10. O-rings
  11. Pins
  12. Rings
  13. Rivets
  14. Spacers
  15. Washers
  16. Wire and cable
