Super Alloy

AEHI is an expert source for a variety of super alloys in bar, plate, sheet, foil, tube, and wire. We have established excellent long-term relationships with major mills, vendors and processors. Make us your one-stop shop for all your aerospace, aircraft and defense industry alloys. Our high quality alloys, product knowledge and competitive prices make us a leader in exotic, high-temperature and super alloys.
